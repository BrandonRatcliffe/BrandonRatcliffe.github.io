About

Personal timeline

  • 2012-2016

    Air Training Core

    I joined the Air Cadets at age 13 and attained the senior rank of Flight Sergeant, leading various groups of individuals from my local squadron and county (Hertfordshire and Buckinghamshire). I took part in a multitude of sporting events, worked in RAF air bases as part of a STEM scheme, shot rifles, and even flew an airplane! The ATC taught me a lot about discipline, comradery, achievment through adversity, & built the foundations of the person I am today. I will always carry fond memories of my time in the cadets, and would recommend it to any young individuals seeking endless opportunity.

  • 2015-Present

    Work & Developing Interests

    After enrolling in the Duke of Edinburgh scheme with the ATC, I volunteered as a Sports Coach at one of my local primary schools. This sprouted into a full time Job, where I would coach my own classes
